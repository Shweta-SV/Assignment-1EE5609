\large Question
\\

Find the cross product of a and b.
$
\\
\\
\vec{a} = \myvec{1\\-7\\7}
$
$
\\
\\
\\
\vec{b} = \myvec{3\\-2\\2}
$
\\
\\
\large Solution =
\\
Cross Product of two vectors- 
\\
\\
Cross product of two vectors is determined by spanning a vector into skew symmetric matrix-
\\
$a \times b$
\\
\\
$
= \begin{bmatrix}
   0 & -a3 & a2\\
   a3 & 0 & -a1\\
   -a2 & a1 & 0\\
\end{bmatrix}
$
$
\myvec{3\\-2\\2}
$
\\
\\
\\
$
= \begin{bmatrix}
  0 & -7 & -7\\
  7 & 0 & -1\\
  7 & 1 & 0\\
\end{bmatrix}
$
